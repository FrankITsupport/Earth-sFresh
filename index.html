Earth's Freshwater is dedicated to providing purified and refined drinking water that is processed using the latest state of hydro-technology, which involves ultraviolet (UV) purification and highly advanced treatment by Reverse Osmosis (RO) machines.

How we purify

Ten stages.
One pure result.

Every drop follows a controlled treatment journey that removes sediment, odours, dissolved impurities, and microorganisms before it reaches the bottle.

Filtration
Treatment & finishing
01
Source

Raw water

Water enters our controlled purification system.

02
Coarse filtration

Quartz sand

Traps sediment, suspended solids, and visible particles.

03
Taste & odour

Activated carbon

Absorbs chlorine, odours, tastes, and organic compounds.

04
Fine filtration

Precision filter

Captures finer particles before membrane treatment.

05
Membrane treatment

RO system

Removes dissolved salts, metals, and contaminants.

06
Balance

Mineralisation

Balances essential minerals for quality and taste.

07
Protected holding

Pure water tank

Stores purified water hygienically before finishing.

08
Light treatment

UV disinfection

Neutralises bacteria without adding chemicals.
